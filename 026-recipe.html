Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic recipe that's quick to make and hard to mess up. Roughly 30 minutes from start to first bite.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar in a separate bowl.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry, then fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions onto a baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 12 minutes or until the edges turn golden.

Top 3 Chef's Tips (Countdown)

  1. Don't open the oven before the timer goes off.
  2. Use room-temperature butter, not melted, not cold.
  3. Read the entire recipe before starting.
