My First HTML Page

A week into learning HTML, here's what I've built and what surprised me along the way.

What I Learned

HTML is less about memorizing tags and more about choosing the right one for the content. Every paragraph is a paragraph; every heading is a heading. Naming the parts is half the battle.

What Tripped Me Up

Forgetting closing tags. Mixing up inline and block elements. Reaching for div when there was a better tag waiting. All things I now catch in seconds, but every one of them slowed me down the first week.

What I'm Doing Next

Building a real personal page from scratch using only semantic tags. No divs unless I genuinely have no better option.
