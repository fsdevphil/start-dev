Weekend in Lisbon

A three-day plan with everything we want to see and the answers to questions friends keep asking.

Itinerary

  1. Friday:
    • Check in to the hotel
    • Walk along the Tagus river
    • Dinner in Alfama.
  2. Saturday:
    • Belém tower tour
    • Pastel de nata tasting
    • Sunset at Miradouro da Senhora do Monte.
  3. Sunday:
    • Tram 28 ride
    • Lunch at Time Out Market
    • Flight home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the trip?
Three full days, Friday to Sunday.
Best month to go?
April or October, warm but not crowded.
Do we need to rent a car?
No, the city is walkable and the metro covers everywhere we listed.
