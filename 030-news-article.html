Local Library Reopens After Two-Year Renovation

Published on March 14, 2025

The Riverside Public Library reopened its doors this week after a two-year renovation that doubled its reading-room capacity and added a dedicated children's wing. The project came in under budget and slightly ahead of schedule.

Visitors on opening day described the space as airy and bright. New study pods, a small recording studio, and a community kitchen now live where the old basement archives used to be.

We didn't just want to modernize the building. We wanted to change what a library can be for the people who walk in.

Maria Lopez, Library Director's Address

Key Takeaways

  • Reading-room capacity doubled.
  • New children's wing on the second floor.
  • Recording studio and community kitchen open to all members.
  • Project came in under budget.
